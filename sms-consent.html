How We Collect Consent

Arc Badlands Compute LLC collects opt-in consent through the following documented methods. This page serves as proof-of-consent documentation for our telecommunications carrier (Twilio Inc.) and regulatory compliance purposes.

Method 1: Keyword Opt-In via SMS

Individuals may text any of the following keywords to (866) 736-2349 to opt in:

JOIN START YES SUBSCRIBE STOP STOPALL UNSUBSCRIBE CANCEL HELP INFO

Upon texting JOIN or START, the individual receives an immediate opt-in confirmation:

Opt-In Confirmation (sent automatically) From: (866) 736-2349 You're now subscribed to Arc Badlands Compute LLC updates. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Msg&data rates may apply. https://claude.ai/public/artifacts/20c1a4ca-b775-4704-be09-9ce6c8994e50

Method 2: Website Contact Form

When a visitor submits their phone number through arcbadlandscompute.com, the contact form includes an explicit SMS consent checkbox (unchecked by default). Checking the box and submitting constitutes consent. The form displays the following disclosure:

Web Form Consent Disclosure (displayed at point of submission) ☐ By providing my phone number and checking this box, I consent to receive automated text messages and/or calls from Arc Badlands Compute LLC at this number about its services. Consent is not required to purchase. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Privacy Policy · SMS Terms

Method 3: Verbal Consent During Call

During inbound or outbound calls, Arc Badlands representatives or its voice AI agent (Audry Harper) may request verbal consent to send follow-up SMS. Verbal consent is logged in our CRM with timestamp, agent ID, and call recording reference.

What We Do Not Send

  • Bulk promotional blasts to lists of individuals who have not opted in
  • Third-party marketing or affiliate promotions
  • Messages to individuals on the national Do Not Call Registry (for consumer numbers)
  • Messages after a STOP request has been received
  • Age-gated, adult, or regulated content
